About Our Services

Groveman can be the backbone of any company that wants to succeed and reach their goals. Our experienced HR and procurement team are equipped to provide your organisation with any type of resource necessary to attain your production needs and profitability. We specialise in Contract Staffing, Talent Acquisition, Executive Hiring, Payroll Management, Statutory Compliances, Skill Development and Sales/Marketing Training.

All industries that need immediate hiring for short term or work on contract can count on us for the right talent or labour that is pre-verified and has gone through a full background screening revealing their identity and ID proof to ensure actual residency and prevent fraudulent activity.

We can take the burden of recruiting and staffing off your shoulders, giving you more time to focus on your business. We have an excellent offer and are equipped to provide a personalised solution quickly. Our network enables us to select candidates that meet your specifications with up to 90% accuracy. Additionally, it helps us keep our overhead costs low.

At Groveman Global Pvt. Ltd., we take care of your payroll needs with competency. Our experienced payroll team has at least 5 years' experience in the field, and ensures the timely delivery of payments to workers, as well as submission and processing of legally required documents. Additionally, staff will be privy to our clear escalation matrix for any queries and our exclusive customer support team is always on-hand to provide individualised attention.

We understand how difficult it can be to create a captivating resume. That's why our team provides professional writing and optimization services to give our customers an edge. Experienced writers collaborate with each client to craft an individualized resume that showcases their talents, successes, and background. Additionally, we make sure resumes pass through applicant tracking systems (ATS) by optimizing them for the initial screening process so that hiring decision-makers can see them.

We boast a comprehensive network with educational institutions in the state; organizing campus interviews commencing in February annually. Our committed team is responsible for maintaining relations with nearly 450 colleges, including 120 women-only institutes. Furthermore, we actively engage in numerous job fairs hosted by district heads, employment exchanges, National Skill Development Centres, Indian Youth employment schemes and NGO programs.
