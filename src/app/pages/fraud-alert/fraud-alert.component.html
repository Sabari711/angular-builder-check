Fraud Alert for Groveman Global

Dear Valued Job Seekers,

It has come to our attention that fraudulent activities target job seekers, falsely claiming association with Groveman Global. To protect our applicants, we issue this fraud alert:

  • No Payment Requests :Groveman Global does not require any payment from job seekers for recruitment processes.
  • Official Channels Only :All communication regarding job applications from Groveman Global will be conducted through official channels. Beware of any communication from unauthorized sources claiming to represent our company.
  • Bogus Recruitment Documentation :Fraudsters may request the completion of fake recruitment documentation via email, such as application forms. Be cautious of such requests as they are not legitimate.
  • Verification Process :Before engaging in any recruitment process or providing personal information, verify the authenticity of the communication and the individual representing Groveman Global.
  • Report Suspicious Activities :If you encounter any suspicious activities claiming to be associated with Groveman Global, please report them immediately to our official email address or contact number.

Groveman Global is committed to ensuring a safe and transparent recruitment process for all our applicants. We appreciate your cooperation and vigilance in helping us maintain the integrity of our recruitment procedures.

Sincerely,

Groveman Global
